Hello everyone, I'm Jordan Belfort. Being a Wall Street broker has been my life. I was a powerful and successful broker who made millions of dollars a year. I lived a life of luxury, driving luxury cars, flying private jets and traveling all over the world. But, as you know, all good things come to an end. I started abusing drugs and alcohol and my life started to go downhill. I lost all the money I made and ended up in jail for fraud and money laundering. I spent several years in prison, where I thought about my life and decided to change. After my release, I started again. I became involved in training and motivation to help others not make the same mistakes that I did. I wrote the book "The Wolf of Wall Street" where I shared my experience and the lessons I learned from my mistakes. I am sure that every person can change his life for the better if he works on himself and is not afraid to make mistakes. I am grateful that I was able to get through the hardships and become the best version of myself. I hope my experience will help someone change their life for the better. Thanks for listening to me.
